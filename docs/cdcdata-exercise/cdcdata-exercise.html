Data Discription

This dataset that I have found on CDC website has collected the pregnant people along weeks in about 2 years from 18 to 49 years old who has received COVID-19 vaccination before and during the pregnancy overall in the United States sorting by race/ethnicity . The original purpose of this data collection is to test the safety of COVID-19 vaccination among pregnant people.
